Opportunities you get as a Tabla Player.

As a tabla player, it is known by tradition that a complete tabla player must fulfill 3 distinct qualities:

  • A good student
  • A good performer
  • A good teacher

In essence this is the basic tradition and guidelines and hence the part about being a performer the best part is the ability to travel to different places and express your music to different people through concerts.

My Own Solos That I have Uploaded online:

I will be going through chronologically the online records of different concerts and events that I have performed. In, these are videos that I recorded myself at different events I have played. And hope you can see the videos and enjoy them as well.

This is my first big event, where I hosted a full concert of around 40 minutes, and this was a major event, this was actually at an examination board, SAMVED, where I gave my examinations and earned my Bachelors in Tabla, through this organization. This was a great achievement for me and a very specical day hence, I am also sharing this event.

This is one of my first ever events playing with another Indian Classical instrument, called the Sitar. This was a big moment for me accomapnying another instrument requires a lot of understanding and experience and doing this being one of my first is still a great thing for me, and I will never forget this.

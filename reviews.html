Tabla Learning Resources Reviews

The Digital Library by Pt. Swapan Chaudhuri

This online library is a great resource for tabla enthusiasts to learn different rare compositions of all legendary tabla artists throughout history. It also includes videos to help students learn the compositions in a better way, just as a face-to-face teacher would. The library covers various styles such as Punjab Gharana, Delhi Gharana, and Farukhabad Gharana. However, it currently only allows compositions verified through Pt. Swapan Chaudhuri, and doesn't accept contributions from other artists. If the library addressed this, it would be an even greater resource for the tabla community.

Tihaai - Quintessence of Indian Percussive Arts by Shri Prabhakar Betrabet

This book by Shri Prabhakar Betrabet and Shri Sandeep Hattangady is a valuable resource for learning how to compose and play tihais, a fundamental concept in Indian classical music. The book offers a clear and practical approach to mastering this essential skill. It includes a CD with recordings of your teacher's senior students demonstrating the concepts, making it even more helpful for students. However, the title may be a bit misleading, as over half the book discusses your teacher's experiences as a musician rather than focusing solely on tihais. Nevertheless, the content on tihais is excellent and provides valuable insights for tabla players.

Want to learn how to play and succeed in Tabla: Check out these websites!

Tabla Legacy

For tabla enthusiasts, this resource offers comprehensive theoretical knowledge essential for tabla examinations, helping to inspire and further push musical boundaries.

Madhyam (SAMVED)

Known as SAMVED exam board, Madhyam facilitates registration for tabla examinations, catering to students interested in formally testing their tabla skills.

ABGMVM

The first musical examination board in India, offering serious students the opportunity to pursue Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in tabla.

iPassio Tabla Lessons

Provides online tabla classes with personal and group sessions, suitable for students unable to attend in-person lessons.

YouTube Tabla Playlist

A comprehensive playlist for self-taught students, featuring major rhythm patterns and exercises to improve tabla skills.

Lessonface with Pt. Samir Chatterjee

Learn tabla from renowned artist Pt. Samir Chatterjee through live, in-person classes—ideal for serious students looking to deepen their expertise.
